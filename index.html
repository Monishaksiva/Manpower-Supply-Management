Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our services

What types of manpower services do you provide?

+

We provide skilled and semi-skilled workforce for Construction, F&B Services, Cleaning Services, and General Helper roles across various industries in Chennai.

How do you ensure the quality of workers?

+

All our workers undergo thorough background verification, skill assessment, and training programs. We maintain strict quality standards and regular performance evaluations.

What is your pricing structure?

+

Our pricing is competitive and depends on the type of service, duration, and number of workers required. We provide customized quotes based on your specific requirements.

How quickly can you deploy workers?

+

We can deploy skilled workers within 24-48 hours for urgent requirements. For larger projects, we typically need 3-5 days for proper planning and deployment.

Do you provide replacement for underperforming workers?

+

Yes, we provide immediate replacement for workers who don't meet your expectations. Our quality assurance team ensures continuous monitoring and improvement.
